🧑‍🍳 Caldereta Recipe

Ingredients

🥩1 kilo beef, cubed

🧅1 onion, chopped

🧄3 cloves garlic, minced

🍅2 cups tomato sauce

🍅2 tablespoons tomato paste

🥔2 potatoes, cubed

🥕2 carrots, cubed

🌶️1 bell pepper, sliced

🥩1/2 cup liver spread

🧀1/2 cup cheese

🧂Salt and pepper to taste

💧 Water

Step-by-Step Cooking

1. Sauté the garlic and onion in oil.

2. Add the beef and cook until lightly browned.

3. Pour in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.

4. Cover and simmer until the beef is tender.

5. Add the potatoes and carrots, then cook until soft.

6. Mix in the liver spread and cheese.

7. Add the bell pepper, season with salt and pepper.
